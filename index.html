About Albert

Bridging technology and business outcomes for over a decade.

I'm a Technical Project Manager with 11+ years leading complex, cross-functional technology projects across banking, insurance, and telecommunications — delivering everything from treasury accounting platform upgrades and regulatory compliance systems to cloud migrations and mobile banking releases.

My career began as a software engineer at Nokia, where I built C++ features for 3G base station software and eventually grew into a delivery management role leading a team of 14. That hands-on engineering foundation shapes how I work today — I engage with developers and architects at a technical level, which makes me a better communicator, a sharper risk identifier, and a more effective bridge between IT teams and business stakeholders.

Across roles at Nokia, UnionBank, Ernst & Young, AXA, and BDO Unibank, I've consistently delivered results: 50% ETL performance improvement, 70% productivity gain through automation, 25% reduction in delivery interdependency, and 12 major banking releases as Programme Lead. I bring the same structured rigour — RAID logs, RACI matrices, steering committee reporting — to both Waterfall and Agile environments.

Now based in Auckland, New Zealand, I hold a PMP and PSM I and am completing a Masters in Software Engineering. I'm open to senior project management and delivery leadership roles across New Zealand where I can drive meaningful digital transformation.
